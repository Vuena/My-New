Frequently Asked Questions

Term vs. Whole Life Insurance?
Term life provides coverage for a specific period (e.g., 20 years) and is affordable. Whole life covers you for a lifetime and builds cash value but costs significantly more.
How accurate is this estimate?
This tool provides a ballpark figure based on standard underwriting. Actual premiums depend on medical exams and specific insurer criteria.
Can I change coverage later?
Yes, decreasing coverage is usually easy. Increasing it typically requires a new medical review.
